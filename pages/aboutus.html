About us

This website is developed by three young professional and talented developer from Kuliyyah of ICT in IIUM which are Ahmad Zahin Fikri bin Rozlee, Muhamad Farid bin Deraman, and Hamzah bin Uddin.

We all know that football is one of the most popular sport in the world. Over 100 million people watch and play the sport.

So we take an initiative to develop a website to provide information, analysis, statistics and reviews from the top legues in the world which are English Premier League, Bundesliga, La liga and Serie A.

Those four leagues have millions fans from all around the globe. Most of the fans had an issue to get up-to-date from those leagues.

In our website we provide many features to the fans that they cannot find elsewhere. We have complete information about those four leagues just in one page. We give hightlight, result and fixtures to ensure the fans will not miss any games.

So we hope the football fans will enjoy our website and any suggestion or improvement from the visitors are allowed to ensure our football websites is the best football website in the world
