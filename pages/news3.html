'Ronaldo Has Been Working Like Crazy!'

After failing to score in his first three matches for the Bianconeri, the Portugal talisman has found great form at the Old Lady.One of the 33-year-old's teammate, Blaise Matuidi, has now explained how Ronaldo has been "an example" to the rest of the team since joining.

"We were all dead tired after the match against Manchester United and the day after, except Cristiano," he told RMC Sport."He's been working like crazy ever since he got here. He's been at it more than anyone else and he told me he 'had to' behave like that.

"That's why at 33, he's been an example for all of us and given us an extra stimulus. His influence isn't limited to his work during the week."He's also crucial over the 90 minutes. He can do things that others can't to help improve the team's game.

"Everyone feels much stronger alongside him. He's a leader in this sense."

Ronaldo has scored seven goals and assisted five strikes in 12 matches for Juve so far this season.
